- SameAs links after Facebook, Google Business Profile, and booking URLs are confirmed.

## 8. Image Direction and Alt Text Recommendations

Replace temporary stock and CSS placeholders with approved real project media:

- Hero: clean Kansas City home or commercial exterior after service. Alt: "Kansas City home exterior after professional pressure washing by Elite Pressure Pros."
- Driveway proof: real before/after concrete. Alt: "Before and after driveway cleaning in the Kansas City Metro Area."
- House wash proof: real siding result. Alt: "Soft washing result on Kansas City home siding."
- Commercial proof: storefront, restaurant, or walkway. Alt: "Commercial pressure washing for Kansas City storefront entrance."
- Team/equipment: owner, truck, surface cleaner, soft-wash setup. Alt should describe the actual scene, not stuff keywords.

## 9. Mobile UX Recommendations

- Keep sticky bottom CTA live on every page.
- Keep forms single-column on mobile.
- Compress and convert approved images to WebP.
- Use tap targets of at least 44px.
- Keep hero copy within first viewport on common mobile sizes.
- Avoid placing city-page links until locations are confirmed.

- Confirm exact service list, especially roof cleaning, gutter cleaning, and window cleaning.
- Confirm service-area coverage and city-page targets.

## 12. Facebook and Owner Verification Notes

Public web search did not return a reliable indexed Facebook page during build, so the site intentionally avoids quoting Facebook captions, using unapproved photos, inventing testimonials, inventing offers, or adding review schema. Before publishing, review the official Facebook page and extract repeatedly mentioned services, project photos, customer comments, service areas, seasonal offers, commercial work, residential proof, booking links, tone of voice, and verified claims.
